# Price Action — Al Brooks Core Concepts / 价格行为核心概念

**Hand-written summary for AlphaLoom RAG. Original paraphrase of widely-taught
price-action ideas, not copied from any third-party corpus.**

## English

Al Brooks reads bar-by-bar price action and classifies the market into a small
number of states. The market is always in one of two modes: a trend or a trading
range, with breakouts as the transition between them.

Trend: a sequence of higher highs and higher lows (bull) or lower highs and lower
lows (bear). In a strong trend, pullbacks are shallow and traders join with the
trend rather than fading it. A trend is a spectrum from strong (few pullbacks,
big trend bars) to weak (deep overlapping pullbacks that resemble a range).

Trading range: price oscillates between support and resistance, and probability
favors reversion. Buy low, sell high, and fade extremes. Most breakout attempts
out of a range fail and get pulled back inside.

Breakout: a strong move out of a range or the start of a new trend. A breakout is
confirmed by follow-through — one or more strong trend bars closing beyond the
level. Weak breakouts become failed breakouts and often reverse.

Pullback entries H1/H2/L1/L2: in a bull leg after a breakout, the first bar that
makes a new high above the prior bar's high is the High 1 (H1) entry; the second
such attempt after another dip is High 2 (H2). H2 is a classic buy in a bull
trend, especially in a two-legged pullback. L1/L2 are the mirror sell setups in a
bear trend. The idea is to enter with the trend on a shallow, structured pullback
rather than chasing the extreme.

## 中文

Al Brooks 逐根 K 线解读价格行为，把市场归入少数几种状态。市场永远处于两种模式之一：
趋势或区间，突破是两者之间的过渡。

趋势：一连串更高的高点与更高的低点（多头），或更低的高点与更低的低点（空头）。强趋势
里回调很浅，交易者顺势加入而非逆势去做反向。趋势是一个从强（回调少、趋势 K 线大）到
弱（回调深且重叠、形似区间）的连续谱。

区间：价格在支撑与阻力之间来回震荡，概率偏向回归。低买高卖、在极端处做反向。区间里
多数突破尝试都会失败并被拉回区间内。

突破：从区间中强力脱出，或一段新趋势的起点。突破由跟随确认——一根或多根强趋势 K 线
收在关键价位之外。弱突破会变成失败突破，常常反转。

回调入场 H1/H2/L1/L2：突破后的一段多头腿里，第一根创出前一根高点上方新高的 K 线是
High 1（H1）入场；再一次回落后的第二次这样的尝试是 High 2（H2）。H2 是多头趋势里的
经典买点，尤其在两腿回调中。L1/L2 是空头趋势里对称的卖出结构。核心思想是在浅而有
结构的回调里顺势入场，而不是在极端处追单。
